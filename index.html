Guess the Number Game

Guess the Number is a simple yet exciting number-guessing game where players have five attempts to identify the correct number between 1 and 100. Each guess brings you closer to the answer with helpful hints that guide you whether the hidden number is higher or lower. Test your luck, sharpen your logic, and see if you can guess the right number to win the prize!
